# Gmail Compose

Helps you compose and send emails through Gmail's web interface.

## Triggers
- "compose email to john@example.com"
- "send email to team@company.com with subject Hello"
- "write email to [someone]"
- "send mail to [recipient]"

## Example Commands
- "Compose an email to john@example.com with subject Hello and say hi"
- "Send an email to team@company.com with a summary of today's meeting"
- "Email sarah@example.com to schedule a meeting next week"

## How It Works
1. Opens Gmail in Chrome or the default browser
2. Clicks the Compose button
3. Fills in recipient, subject, and body
4. Sends the email

## Notes
- Works with Gmail web app in any browser
- Handles multiple recipients if comma-separated
- Ask for confirmation before sending if the email is important
